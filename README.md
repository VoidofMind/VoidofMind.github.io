			<h1 id="firstHeading" class="firstHeading" lang="en">Dr. Akaggy</h1>
									<div id="bodyContent" class="mw-body-content">
									<div id="siteSub">From Starbounder - Starbound Wiki</div>

				<div id="mw-content-text" lang="en" dir="ltr" class="mw-content-ltr"><div class="floatright"><a href="/File:DrAkaggy.jpg" class="image" title="Dr. Akaggy, Mech Scientist"><img alt="Dr. Akaggy, Mech Scientist" src="/mediawiki/images/8/8d/DrAkaggy.jpg" width="350" height="287" /></a></div>
<p><strong class="selflink">Dr. Akaggy</strong> is a <a href="/Penguin" title="Penguin">penguin</a> scientist who has set up shop at the the <a href="/Outpost" title="Outpost">Outpost</a>. She specialises in constructing <a href="/Mech" title="Mech" class="mw-redirect">Mechs</a> and will generously reward those who help her in testing her newest models.
</p><p>Assuming they survive, of course.
</p><p><br />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Encounters">Encounters</span></h2>
<p>Dr. Akaggy's workshop opens up once the <a href="/Player" title="Player">player</a> has completed the "<a href="/Become_Space-worthy" title="Become Space-worthy">Become Space-worthy</a>" <a href="/Quest" title="Quest" class="mw-redirect">quest</a>, finally allowing proper space travel. This also gives the player access to the <a href="/Mech_Assembly_Station" title="Mech Assembly Station">Mech Assembly Station</a> and <a href="/Mech_Part_Crafting_Table" title="Mech Part Crafting Table">Mech Part Crafting Table</a>, though they can't do anything with the stations until they unlock more <a href="/Mechs#Mech_Components" title="Mechs">mech blueprints</a> and gather the right <a href="/Category:Salvage_Component" title="Category:Salvage Component">salvage components</a>. Completing her first side-quest grants the parts to do so, and she'll offer even more quests to further upgrade the player's mech.
</p>
<table class="gametable">
<tr>
<td style="width:240px;vertical-align:top;">
<p><a href="/Test_Drive" title="Test Drive">Test Drive</a><br />
<a href="/Parts_and_Labour" title="Parts and Labour">Parts and Labour</a>
</p>
</td>
<td>
<p><a href="/Penguin_Weaponry_Testing_Site" title="Penguin Weaponry Testing Site">Penguin Weaponry Testing Site</a><br />
<a href="/Advanced_Test_Drive" title="Advanced Test Drive">Advanced Test Drive</a>
</p>
</td></tr></table>
<p>She is also the pilot of the <a href="/Shockhopper_Mk_I" title="Shockhopper Mk I">Shockhopper Mk I</a> mech battled at the Penguin Weaponry Testing Site. Beating her unlocks a new mech <a href="/Mechs#Mech_Components" title="Mechs">mech blueprints</a>, and also opens up <a href="/Biggy%27s_Reputable_Weaponry" title="Biggy's Reputable Weaponry">Biggy's Reputable Weaponry</a> across the hall from her workshop.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Trivia">Trivia</span></h2>
<ul><li>Dr. Akaggy may be named after <a rel="nofollow" target="_blank" class="external text" href="https://wiki.evageeks.org/Naoko_Akagi">Dr. Naoko Akagi</a> or <a rel="nofollow" target="_blank" class="external text" href="https://wiki.evageeks.org/Ritsuko_Akagi">Dr. Ritsuko Akagi</a>from the anime <i>Neon Genesis Evangelion</i>.</li></ul>
